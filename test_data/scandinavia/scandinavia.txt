# Scandinavian Countries

Scandinavia primarily includes Denmark, Norway, and Sweden. The broader Nordic region also includes Finland and Iceland.

## Geography
The Scandinavian Peninsula is Europe's largest peninsula, featuring dramatic landscapes with fjords, mountains, and forests.

## Culture
Scandinavian countries are known for high standards of living, social welfare systems, design, and cultural values emphasizing equality.

## Languages
Danish, Norwegian, and Swedish are North Germanic languages and mutually intelligible. Finnish belongs to the Finno-Ugric family.
